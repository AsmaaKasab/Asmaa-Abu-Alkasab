My Favorite Animal: Elephant

The elephant is the largest land animal. They are smart and very social.

Why I Love Elephants

  • They are gentle giants.
  • They have great memories.
  • They live in families and care for each other.

Fun Facts About Elephants

  • Elephants can weigh up to 6 tons!
  • They can talk to each other using low sounds.
  • Elephants use their trunks for many things, like drinking water.

Habitat

Elephants live in many places like savannas, forests, and grasslands. They need a lot of space to find food.

How You Can Help Elephants

You can help protect elephants by supporting groups that save wildlife and telling others how important they are.
