Top 5 Adventure Destinations

Pack your bags (well, your imagination) and get ready to travel to these amazing places:

  1. Cedar Forest : Explore the largest lebanese jungle filled with exotic animals and plants.
  2. Byblos : It is considered one of the oldest cities in the world, and contains many ancient monuments such as the Roman port and the ancient Byblos Castle. The city also has beautiful beaches and great restaurants.
  3. Faraya Resort : Climb the tallest lebanese mountain (but wear a coat, it's cold up there!).
  4. Baalbek Castle : historic castle considered one of the oldest castles in Lebanon.
  5. Jeita Grotto : it is one of the deepest waterways in the world. It is considered one of the wonders of global nature.

Fun Adventures To Try At Home

You don't need to leave home to have an adventure. Try one of these awesome activities:

  • Create your own treasure map and go on a scavenger hunt
  • Make your own mini volcano with baking soda and vinegar
  • Draw and design your own superhero costume
