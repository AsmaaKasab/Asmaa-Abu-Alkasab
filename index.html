Yummy Orange Cake Recipe!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup of orange juice
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • A pinch of salt

How to Make It

  1. First, turn on the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a big bowl, mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add the butter and mix it until it looks crumbly.
  4. Add the eggs and orange juice. Mix everything together!
  5. Pour the batter into a greased cake pan.
  6. Bake it in the oven for about 30-35 minutes.
  7. When it is golden brown, take it out and let it cool. Enjoy your yummy cake!

Why I Love This Cake!

This orange cake is super yummy! It’s sweet and tastes like oranges. Perfect for parties or just because!
